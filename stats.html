What do these numbers mean?
Evening gowns viewed
How many times someone looked closely at a gown — opening its photo, plus flipping through its colours. The small line on the card splits the two.
Men suits viewed
The same thing on the suits side.
Visits
How many times the website was opened. The same person opening it twice counts twice, so read it as traffic, not as people.
Total reach-outs
Every time a customer started a conversation: chat button opens + price enquiries + booking clicks. This is the number to watch — it's the closest thing to a lead.
Chat button opens
Taps on the general "chat with us" buttons — the floating WhatsApp button, the one in the Book-a-fitting section, and the showroom notice. Not tied to any piece.
Price enquiries
Taps on "Ask price" inside a piece's photo popup. Always tied to a specific piece, which is what makes it the strongest signal on the page.
Booking clicks
"Book a fitting" on a piece, or sending the booking form.
Most viewed pieces
What customers opened most in this date range. The coloured dots under each row show which colours of that piece they actually looked at.
Most wanted colours
Colour popularity across the whole catalogue, not per piece.
What people searched
The exact words customers typed into the search box, most frequent first. A word that keeps coming up for something you don't carry is a demand signal.
Which filters they used
Which colours, styles, fabrics and brands customers tapped in the filter panel — what they were actively looking for.
Rental questions opened
Which rental FAQ questions customers expanded — deposit, fitting fee, cleaning, and so on. Heavy interest in one question tells you what's on people's minds before they book.
Highest intent — price enquiries
Views against price enquiries for each piece. A high ask rate means people who see it want it — worth restocking. Many views with no enquiries usually means the photo pulls people in but the piece doesn't close.

Not counted: scrolling past pieces, switching between the Gowns and Suits tabs, and using the filters. Only deliberate actions are recorded.

The numbers above are for the selected date range. Use the Pieces and What they wanted tabs for the detail behind them.
