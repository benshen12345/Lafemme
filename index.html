About the Studio

Dressed for the moment.

LaFemme Gowns by Nee is a gown and suit rental studio in Pusat Bandar Bukit Jalil, Kuala Lumpur. We keep a curated wardrobe of evening gowns and men's suits for the occasions that matter — annual dinners, galas, convocations and ROM ceremonies.

Rather than buying a gown you'll wear once, rent one that fits you properly. Visits are by appointment, so the fitting room is yours alone.

Good to Know

Rental questions, answered

How long is the rental period?
The standard rental period is 4 days. If you need an extended period, an extension surcharge applies — message us on WhatsApp for the extension rates and details.
How much does it cost to rent?
Rental prices start from RM300+ — each piece is priced by its design and detailing. Send us a piece code on WhatsApp for its exact rental price, or visit our showroom and we'll help you find your dream outfit.
Is there a fitting fee?
Yes — RM10 per person, per hour. If you rent an outfit during your fitting appointment, the fitting fee is fully waived. Need extra fitting time? Just message us for the applicable charges.
Is there a deposit?
Yes — a security deposit is collected when you pick up your outfit, and fully refunded once it's returned in good condition with no damage, permanent stains, or missing items. It secures your piece throughout the rental period.
What about cleaning and alterations?
Dry-cleaning is included — return the piece as-is and we handle the rest. Basic fit-pinning is done free in-studio; more complex alterations are quoted individually.
How early should I book?
As early as possible — our gowns are first come, first served. Most clients book around 3 months before their event to secure their preferred gown. Once you've found the one, we recommend confirming early to ensure it's available for your special day.
