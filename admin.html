How it works

Changes here go live on the website by themselves. No zip, no re-uploading to Netlify.

To add a piece
  1. Go to Add New.
  2. Fill in the name, pick the style and fabric tags.
  3. For each colour, choose the colour and upload one photo.
  4. Hit Save piece — it uploads and goes straight onto the site.
  5. Open the website (or refresh it) after a few seconds to see it.
To fix an existing piece — open Catalogue, click Edit on any piece, change its style/fabric tags or colours, then save. Good for correcting the pieces that were auto-tagged.
To add a new filter — open Filters to add your own Style, Fabric or Colour (no photo needed). It becomes tickable in the Add/Edit form right away, and shows on the website once a piece uses it.
Good to know
• Use the best-quality photo you have. Avoid photos sent over WhatsApp — it shrinks them.
• Photos are auto-resized into a grid thumbnail and a full-size version.
• Deleting a piece removes it from the site immediately.
• If you don't replace a colour's photo while editing, the existing photo stays.
